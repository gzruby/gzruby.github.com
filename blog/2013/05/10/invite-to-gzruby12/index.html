GZRUBY#12 May 15 - 7:30PM - Deployment

May 10th, 2013

Ruby comrades! It is time to again unite, to share our secrets, and to push forth into the cold, cruel world of web development. This month’s gzruby meetup will be held next Wednesday, May 15th at the Kudelabs offices, starting at 7:30.

各位，两个月过去了，是时候再次聚会了！本月的gzruby meetup将会在2013年5月15号（下个星期三）7点半在酷德实验室准时举行。

Since the last time we met, Rails 4.0 RC1 is out! Has anyone started using it yet? How are we doing on Ruby 2.0? And wow, we haven’t had a security update in 2 months, that’s good news.

自从上次我们聚会后，Rails 4.0 RC1 来啦! 有没有人开始使用它呢？Ruby 2.0用的怎么样啦？还有一个好消息是，我们已经两个月没有安全性更新。

At this meetup, lets talk Deployment! We’ve all been there - VPS accounts, Cloud Hosting, App Engines. What’s working for you guys? What’s the best rails host in China? Cap or Vlad? Bring us your ideas, your ninja methods, your sysadmin hacks - its time to share and learn from each other!

这次聚会上，我们的主题是Deployment！我们都有着使用 VPS accounts, Cloud Hosting, App Engines的经历。你们觉得它们怎么样？中国最好的Rails Host是哪一个？Cap还是Vlad？来这里交流你的ideas，ninja methods和sysadmin hacks吧。

时间/When: 星期三－5月15号 19:30 / May 15, 7:30 PM

地点/Where: Kudelabs 明月一路59号, 汉苑 西塔601

59 Ming Yue Yi Lu, Han Yuan West Tower, Apt. 601

地铁/Metro: 五羊村A出口/Line 5 - Wu Yang Cun, Exit A

We look forward to seeing you there! If you have any topic ideas or plans, let us know on the mailing list.

希望到时能见到你的身影！如果你有什么好的想法，发到邮件列表告诉我们吧!

关于gzruby

gzruby是由中国广州Ruby开发者组成的一个团体。如果你对Ruby编程语言或者Ruby on Rails网站开发框架， 甚至只是单纯地对网站开发感兴趣，都欢迎加入我们！
gzruby is a group for ruby developers in Guangzhou, China. It is open to anyone interested in the ruby programming language, the Ruby on Rails web development framework, or even web development in general.

我们初定每隔一个月的第三个周三举行一次聚会。最新聚会或者相关活动的详细信息，我们都会事先在此公布。 欲了解更多详情，请订阅我们的邮件列表。
We try to meet on the third wednesday, every other month. We'll post announcements here for upcoming meetings or other events. If you want to learn more, please join our mailing list.
